# Meeting Notes

## Purpose

This folder stores meeting notes that capture discussions, action items, and follow-ups.

## What Belongs Here

- Meeting notes
- Discussion summaries
- Action item tracking notes
- Follow-up references from working sessions

## Naming Conventions

- Use dates, meeting names, or session identifiers.
- Keep filenames consistent so notes are easy to sort chronologically.

## Rules

- Do not turn meeting notes into authoritative decisions unless they are later formalized elsewhere.
- Keep notes concise and traceable.
- Separate meeting notes from ADRs, source documents, and final knowledge summaries.
