# Product Knowledge

## Purpose

This folder stores product-oriented knowledge that helps explain what the repository is building and why.

## What Belongs Here

- Product behavior summaries
- User-facing workflow notes
- Scope and non-scope clarifications
- Product-level implications of source material

## Naming Conventions

- Use product capability names or lifecycle topics.
- Keep names focused on the product outcome.

## Rules

- Do not duplicate source documents.
- Do not store architecture decisions unless they are needed to explain product behavior.
- Keep the content compact and future-friendly.
