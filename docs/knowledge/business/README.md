# Business Knowledge

## Purpose

This folder stores business-domain understanding distilled from source documents.

## What Belongs Here

- Business rules
- Product intent summaries
- Stakeholder-facing domain notes
- Business terminology interpretations

## Naming Conventions

- Use business capability names or topic names.
- Prefer stable names that reflect the subject matter.

## Rules

- Do not include raw source text here.
- Do not add implementation details unless they are part of business understanding.
- Keep entries readable, structured, and durable.
