# Research

## Purpose

This folder stores research notes that inform future documentation, architecture, or product decisions.

## What Belongs Here

- Investigation notes
- Comparative research
- External reference analysis
- Exploratory findings that are not yet decisions

## Naming Conventions

- Use topic-based names with clear scope.
- Prefer names that show the research question or subject.

## Rules

- Do not present research as a final decision.
- Keep research separate from ADRs and source material.
- Record enough context for future reuse without overexplaining.
